We aim to create a Ragging-free environment for every student.

An instituition will only succeed when no student feels repressed or unsafe and is able to express themselves freely and confidently.
Ragging refers to an act of disorderly conduct, directed towards a student of an educational institution which causes or likely to cause physical or physiological harm or raising apprehension or fear or shame or embarrassment to that student.

Effects of Ragging

1. The victims of ragging suffer maximum in forms of depression, isolation, demoralization and many other forms of detrimental effects on the personality.
2. In extreme cases, the victim can even commit suicide due to the mental pressures that develop over a period of time.
3. If ragging is not terminated at any point, the entire learning atmosphere is vitiated.
4. There will be a constant fear and tension in the mind of every second student that she/he might be the next victim of ragging.
5. The free and liberal atmosphere in learning tends to vanish in such colleges or institutes.

How can we stop Ragging?

Institutes should arrange counseling session for students and fresher’s to speak their mind.Anti-ragging cells should be mandated. A fresher’s party has to be organized by the institutes within a week of the academic session so that students can easily interact. Students must be made aware of the penalties incurred when caught ragging so that they recognize it as a crime. Lastly, all of us should pledge never to rag, support anyone or turn a blind eye towards anyone who participates in this sin. Together we can ensure that every student only takes forward good memories and positivity when they step out of their instituitions.
